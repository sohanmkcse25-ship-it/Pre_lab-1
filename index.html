Akira Toriyama

Creator of Dragon Ball

Life

Akira Toriyama was born in 1955 in Japan. He first became popular with Dr. Slump and later created Dragon Ball, which made him world famous.

Works

Dr. Slump (1980) – comedy manga.

Dragon Ball (1984–1995) – legendary shonen manga and anime.

Character designs for Dragon Quest and Chrono Trigger games.

Legacy

Toriyama’s art inspired generations. His characters like Goku and Arale are loved worldwide.
